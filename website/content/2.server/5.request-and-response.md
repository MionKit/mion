mion uses its own `MionRequest` and `MionResponse` objects. Unlike other modern frameworks, mion does not use the native `Request` and `Response` objects. 

This way it is easier to adapt mion to different platforms and environments while keeping a good performance across different platforms.

You can access them from the [Call Context](/docs/server/call-context) as `ctx.request` and `ctx.response`.
